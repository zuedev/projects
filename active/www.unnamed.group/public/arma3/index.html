Unnamed Arma Group

We're a no-BS milsim community formed on the idea of real fun with friends. We don't do training or exams, and we definitely don't do "yes sir, no sir." We focus on small-team tactics and play a whole bunch of games including Star Citizen and Dungeons & Dragons.

Our schedule right now is quite sporadic, but we aim to play an Arma 3 session every 2-3 weeks or so. We use Discord to communicate outside of game but extensively use ACRE2 and TeamSpeak 3 to simulate proximity and radio communications in-game.

If you're interested in joining us, feel free to drop by our Discord and check out when we're playing next. There should also be a whole bunch of other events scheduled between Arma 3 sessions.
